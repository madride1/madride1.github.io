Local Chapter

Parkway PTA is a chapter of the National PTA and we strive to support our students excel at school. We need help from our Parkway Families to become PTA members and support us in our task to help our students. By becoming a PTA member a portion of your membership dues go directly to supporting Parkway students. These funds help support activities that are held throught the school year.

We are open to suggestions and how we can make improvements that will continue to support the students.
